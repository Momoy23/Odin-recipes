Silken Egg

My mom used to make silken egg as a dish when I was little. It is an easy and simple dish.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of water
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Light soy sauce
  • Spring onion for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk eggs, salt and water together.
  2. Cover with aluminium foil.
  3. Steam for 12-15 minutes on high heat until the center is cooked.
  4. Once cooked, add soy sauce on top and garnish with spring onion.

Enjoy!
