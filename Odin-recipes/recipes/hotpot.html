Hotpot

What is Chinese Hot pot?

Chinese Hot Pot is an interactive meal in which diners sit around a simmering pot of soup at the center of the table with various raw ingredients - meat, seafood, vegetables, tofu, and carbs.

Hotpot is enjoyed on winter days. This dish is originated in China.

Equipment needed to have hotpot at home

  • Heat source
  • Pot
  • Chopsticks
  • Metal hot pot baskets/wire ladles

Ingredients to bring to the table

  • Chinese leafy greens - pick as many as you want
  • Mushrooms
  • Thin slices of Beef
  • Thin slices of Pork
  • Thin slices of Pork Belly
  • Shrimp
  • Tofu
  • Frozen dumplings
  • Thin noodles
  • Soy sauce
  • Dipping sauce of your liking
  • Broth
  • Chopped cilantro

Instructions

  1. Place boiling soup base or stock in a wide, shallow pot the center of the table on a portable electric burner
  2. Place individual plates of raw ingredients on the table, along with dipping sauce.
  3. Have each diner mix their own dipping sauce while the pot of soup comes to a boil.
  4. Once boiling, begin adding ingredients to the pot. Be sure to cook ingredients through before consuming.
  5. Allow the pot to boil for at least 30 seconds to 1 minute after adding any raw meat or seafood

    6. Enjoy!
